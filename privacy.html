Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Research Paper Organizer, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our service.

Last Updated: January 2025
Effective Date: January 2025

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you choose to add to your profile
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to us through contact forms or support channels

Research Data

  • Uploaded Files: Research papers, documents, and related files you upload
  • Metadata: Information about your papers including titles, authors, tags, and notes
  • Organization Data: How you organize and categorize your research

Usage Information

  • Activity Data: How you interact with our service and features you use
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device characteristics
  • Log Data: IP addresses, access times, and technical information for security purposes

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our research organization tools
  • Store and organize your research papers and related data
  • Enable features like search, filtering, and categorization
  • Sync your data across different devices and platforms

Communication

  • Send important service updates and security notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send occasional product updates (with your consent)

Security and Legal

  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other policies

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our platform (cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales (with notice)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using strong encryption methods
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication for our systems
  • Regular Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats and vulnerabilities
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosting on secure, certified cloud platforms
  • Data Backups: Regular backups to prevent data loss
Note: While we use industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. We recommend keeping backups of important research data.

Your Rights and Choices

Account Management

  • Access: View and download your personal information and research data
  • Update: Modify your account information and preferences
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your research data in standard formats

Privacy Controls

  • Data Retention: Choose how long to keep your research data
  • Communication Preferences: Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Cookie Settings: Manage cookie preferences in your browser

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in securely
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: Understand how our service is used (anonymized data)
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality.

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion
  • Research Data: Stored according to your preferences, with options for automatic deletion
  • Log Data: Typically retained for 90 days for security and troubleshooting purposes
  • Backup Data: May be retained in secure backups for up to 1 year for disaster recovery

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will notify you by email and through our service
  • The updated policy will be posted on our website
  • Continued use of our service indicates acceptance of the changes
  • Significant changes will include a 30-day notice period

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@researchorganizer.com

We will respond to your privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.
